How It Works

There is no right or wrong way to move forward after experiencing sexual assault — just your way.

What You Can Do with a Record

Choose to Save

You can choose to just save your record - it never has to go anywhere if you don't want it to.

Choose to Match

You can choose to submit only the perpetrator’s information into Survivor Support’s matching system, which identifies repeat offenders.

Choose to Report

You can report to Grinnell by sending them your record through Survivor Support. Grinnell will then reach out to you about next steps.
