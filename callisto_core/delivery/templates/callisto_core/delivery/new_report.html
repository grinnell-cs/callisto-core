Securing Your Record

Before we start the record process we want to make sure we keep it safe.

You will need this passphrase to gain access to your record to view, edit, enter into match, or report the record to your school.
For your safety, we do not store passphrases. If you forget your passphrase, you won't be able to access this record.
We recommend that you either store your passphrase in a secure location or print a PDF of your report for your own records.
