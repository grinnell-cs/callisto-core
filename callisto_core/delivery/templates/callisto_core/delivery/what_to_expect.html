What to Expect

Here's what you need to know before you begin.

Trust is paramount to Surivor Support.
We want to empower you to make the choice that’s right for you.

Your record stays private to you. Please be sure to write down only what you are sure of; if you choose to report this record, this is the information your school will see.

We encrypt each record with your passphrase so it is safe. Even we can't see your record.

We do not send your record to your school unless you choose to report it.

You can come back at any time to finish or update your record.

If you’re not ready to answer a question choose “I’d rather not say” or click “Next” to move forward.

If you choose to report this record to your school, you will need to give your school your contact information so that they can follow up.

If you’d like to enter into our matching system, you will need to have the perpetrator's Facebook URL.
